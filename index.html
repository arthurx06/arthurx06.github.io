About

Macroeconomist and research analyst specializing in regional economic and financial market surveillance for China and Emerging Asia. Based in Hong Kong, I provide analytical coverage of growth, inflation, FX, and sovereign bond markets, utilizing quantitative frameworks to assess policy transmission and real-time market outcomes.

My research portfolio is anchored in international trade and sovereign debt sustainability analysis (DSA) within Emerging Markets. I have a particular interest in quantifying geopolitical risk and its transmission mechanisms through global supply chains and commodity markets, bridging structural research with conjunctural market analysis.

During my three-year tenure as a Research Analyst at the International Monetary Fund, I contributed to sovereign debt policy in the Strategy, Policy, and Review Department and real-time monitoring of trade disruptions in the Asia-Pacific Department. I am dedicated to modernizing institutional research by integrating advanced econometrics (VAR/VECM) with modern data tools and LLM-driven autonomous agents.
